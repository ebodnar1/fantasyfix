Expected Results

Expected results are calculated by comparing each team's weekly score to every other team's score for that week. The team with the highest score for a week will earn 1 expected wins for that week, since they would have beaten any opponent they would have faced. The team with the lowest score for a week will earn 0 expected wins for that week, since they would have lost to any opponent they would have faced. The expected wins are summed for all completed weeks and compared to each team's actual net wins (wins + half credit to losses), which yields the wins above expected value shown. A positive value for wins above expected means a team is getting lucky in their matchups and exceeding expectations, while a negative value means a team is getting unlucky in their matchups, and should be performing better.
